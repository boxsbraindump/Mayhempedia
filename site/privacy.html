Trust note

Privacy & play safety

Mayhempedia is a local desktop companion for ARAM: Mayhem. It is designed to show build, augment, and local match information without taking actions in League of Legends.

What Mayhempedia reads

When the League Client is running, Mayhempedia reads local client state to identify your champion selection and display match history, items, and augments. It does not ask for, capture, or store your Riot account password.

What stays on your computer

Settings, custom routes, selected route preferences, and optional match-history cache are saved locally on your device. This information is not uploaded by Mayhempedia. You can disable local history and remove remembered accounts from the app settings.

What the app does not do

  • It does not read League of Legends game memory.
  • It does not automate gameplay, clicks, purchases, champion actions, or augment choices.
  • Its overlay is display-only. Hotkeys only open, hide, or move Mayhempedia windows.

Updates and feedback

The app checks GitHub Releases for updates after installation. When you choose to send beta feedback, Mayhempedia opens a GitHub issue form in your browser with your optional comment; it does not silently transmit comments or match history.

Questions or concerns

Please report a problem on GitHub. Mayhempedia is fan-made and is not endorsed by Riot Games.
